The platform

The loop between data and decisions

Most tools stop at collecting telemetry and drawing dashboards. axiomloop turns operational data into contextual intelligence that keeps improving — across every system you run.

◈

Observe everything

Treat every connected system as observable — cloud, Kubernetes, Linux, edge, and IoT — unified into one coherent model instead of a wall of disconnected metrics.

⬡

Understand relationships

Model dependencies, causality, topology, and how they change over time — because context lives in the relationships, not in isolated signals.

◇

Learn continuously

Every incident, anomaly, and recovery becomes engineering memory — so each decision is more informed than the last, through a closed feedback loop.

Capabilities

Graph intelligence

Relationships, not just metrics

Every component lives in a network of dependencies. Model topology, causality, ownership, and resource flow — and reason about how they connect.

  • Topology & ownership
  • Relationship-aware analysis
Time-aware intelligence

Temporal knowledge graphs

  • Evolving dependencies

  • Feedback-driven reasoning
  • Outcome-aware predictions
  • Continuous improvement
