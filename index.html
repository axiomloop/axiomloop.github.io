How it works

From signal to certainty in four stages

  1. 01

    Sense

    IoT sensors and RFID readers capture the physical world at the edge — resilient, secure, and always on.

  2. 02

    Compute

    WebAssembly modules process data on-site in milliseconds; eBPF instruments the system itself with zero overhead.

  3. 03

    Validate

    The graph engine models every relationship and continuously proves data integrity, lineage, and consistency.

  4. 04

    Observe

    Unified, correlated telemetry gives your teams real-time answers — from a single sensor to the whole fleet.
